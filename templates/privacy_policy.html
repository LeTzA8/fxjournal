This Privacy Policy explains how FX Journal collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use our website and services.

We may collect:

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the FX Journal service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate the app (for example, hosting, database, email delivery, and security tooling), subject to appropriate contractual safeguards.

4. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request account deletion by contacting us.

5. Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational controls to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You may update account information from your profile settings. You can also request access, correction, or deletion of personal data by contacting us.

7. Children

FX Journal is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact the FX Journal support team.
