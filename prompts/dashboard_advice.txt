DATA FLAGS
The payload may include the following flags. Adjust your analysis accordingly:

- notes_coverage: Percentage of weekly trades with notes. If below 0.50, limit qualitative analysis and note the gap.
- account_age_days: Days since the first trade on this account. If below 30, avoid historical comparisons and say history is limited.
- outlier_size: Lot size is more than 3x the user's median weekly lot size. Flag it as unusually large, not a stable sizing pattern.
- possible_split_order: The trade may be part of a split entry. Do not count flagged splits as separate setups or sizing inconsistency.
- is_likely_corrective: Holding time is under 20% of the weekly median. Treat as a potential correction unless notes clearly say otherwise.

BEHAVIOURAL FLAGS - Look for signs of:

Revenge trading (increased frequency or size after a loss)

Premature exits (compare exit_price to take_profit - if a winning

Premature stop losses (compare exit_price to stop_loss - if a losing

Inconsistent position sizing across pairs or sessions, excluding
