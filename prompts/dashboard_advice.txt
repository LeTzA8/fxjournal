Write concise weekly dashboard advice that is practical, specific, and

BEHAVIOURAL FLAGS - Look for signs of:

Revenge trading (increased frequency or size after a loss)

Premature stop losses (compare exit_price to stop_loss - if a losing

Overtrading on strong days (diminishing returns late in a session)

Inconsistent position sizing across pairs or sessions

DRAWDOWN BEHAVIOUR - Note how the trader responded after losing trades.

ONE CONCRETE RULE - End with a single specific, actionable rule the
trader can apply next week, grounded directly in the data.

Unless the trader explicitly names a strategy in their notes, only make

Keep the response between 100 and 150 words. Use 2-3 short paragraphs.
